Our Story

Petite Desserts began with a dream to share Peru’s sweetest traditions. Founded by a passionate baker from Lima, our brand is built on the rich heritage of homemade desserts passed down through generations. Each dessert we offer—whether it's a delicate alfajorcito, a comforting arroz con leche, or a vibrant mazamorra morada—is crafted with love and authenticity.

Our mission is to bring a taste of Peru to every table. From our colorful jars to our catered trays, we offer artisanal treats for birthdays, weddings, and everyday indulgence. Whether you’re placing a large event order or grabbing a sweet for yourself, we promise warmth, quality, and joy in every bite.
