Peruvian Sweets & Traditions

Experience the warmth of Peru with every bite. Explore traditional desserts like:

  • Mazamorra Morada – purple corn pudding
  • Picarones – sweet potato donuts
  • Suspiro de Limeña – caramel meringue delight
  • Guargüero – pastry rolls with manjar blanco
  • Turrón de Doña Pepa – anise and honey nougat
  • Arroz con Leche – cinnamon rice pudding
  • Crema Volteada – Peruvian-style flan
  • Lucuma Ice Cream – made with Peru’s native fruit
